At Stanford, I was class president, chief of staff of the student government, convocation student speaker, editor-in-chief of Intersect: The Stanford Journal of Science, Technology, and Society, and studied technological entrepreneurship as a Mayfield Fellow. I am a 3rd degree black belt in taekwondo and am passionate about theater. I merge my diverse interests to study human enhancement and pursue global biotechnological entrepreneurship.
