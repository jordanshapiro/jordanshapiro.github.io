ANKLE SPRAIN

I worked with three classmates to create an educational device for patients suffering from ankle sprain. The device combines mechanical and electrical elements to demonstrate the various types and severities of sprains and the associated anatomical impacts. Users can manipulate the base of the device to see how their sprain happened and how to prevent simular injury in the future.
