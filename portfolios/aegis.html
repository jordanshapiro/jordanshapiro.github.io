AEGIS

The Aegis is a Taekwondo board breaking holder. While practicing and instructing Taekwondo classes as a third degree black belt, I recognized two specific problem scenarios for board breaking:

The first was that as a student, I was unable to practice breaking outside of my Taekwondo exams because my friends and family did not know how to safely hold a board or, alternatively, were too scared to do so for fear of being kicked. This left me unprepared for important board breaks during my exams.

The second problem (perhaps a result of the first) I experienced as an instructor who endured countless smashed fingers when my students missed their intended targets. Especially in high-speed, flying, or performance breaks for which martial artists are expected to use more power, inaccurate strikes or sharp board shards can seriously injure board holders.

The Aegis solves for both of these problems by putting distance between the board and the board holder. A shield protects against flying board shards and easily detachable velcro straps hold boards firmly in place.
